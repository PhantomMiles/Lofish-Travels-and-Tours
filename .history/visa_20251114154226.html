General Visa Document Checklist

While requirements differ across embassies, most visa applications typically include:

  • Valid international passport (minimum 6-month validity)
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Completed visa application form
  • Travel itinerary or flight reservation
  • Proof of accommodation / hotel booking
  • Bank statements (3–6 months)
  • Employment letter or business registration
  • Sponsorship or invitation documents (if applicable)
  • Travel insurance
  • Additional country-specific requirements
