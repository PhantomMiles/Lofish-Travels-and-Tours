About Lofish Global Travels & Tours

Your trusted gateway to seamless journeys, global connections, and unforgettable experiences.

Who We Are

Founded in Enugu, Nigeria, Lofish Global Travels & Tours is a premier travel management company providing end-to-end solutions for individuals, families, and corporations. Our expertise covers visa assistance, flight bookings, study abroad guidance, corporate travel management, and international freight logistics.

With an unwavering commitment to trust, transparency, and excellence, we have built long-lasting relationships across Africa, Europe, Asia, and North America. We believe travel should be smooth, safe, and rewarding — and our dedicated team ensures every journey reflects that.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To deliver world-class travel solutions with integrity, professionalism, and innovation — helping clients explore opportunities globally.

Vision: To become Africa’s most trusted travel brand, connecting cultures, businesses, and dreams through exceptional travel experiences.
